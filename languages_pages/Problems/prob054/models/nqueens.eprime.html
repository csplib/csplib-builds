language ESSENCE' 1.0

$ given      n : int(1..)
letting n be 8

letting dom be domain int(1..n)
find x: matrix indexed by [ dom ] of dom
$ branching on x
$    heuristic srf

such that  
   allDiff(x),
   allDiff([x[i]+i | i : dom]),
   allDiff([x[i]-i | i : dom])
