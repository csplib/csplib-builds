For the 2-fractions puzzle, the unique result (up to symmetry) is: 

8    9
-- + -- == 1
26   13
Similarly, the 3-fractions puzzle has a unique result up to symmetry: 
5    7    9
-- + -- + -- == 1
34   68   12
